About.

The Main Suite, an all wood construction, consists of the best and most obscure custom boutique preamps, compressors, reverb, delay and saturation units, top of the line converters and the cleanest, yet lush monitoring chain. It’s designed to be the most natural and precise sonic environment possible, allowing us to make the decisions necessary to make your music sound its best.
