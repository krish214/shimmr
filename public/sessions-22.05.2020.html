Shimmr Sessions with The Score Magazine.

Shimmr Studios, in collaboration with The Score Magazine introduces an interesting endeavour. We make an instrumental/beat and invite artists to put their own spin on it- Sing, rap, play a lead instrument, do a full-on arrangement or add parts to the existing piece. The possibilities are endless.

It’s also an interesting opportunity to see how different people interpret the same source. We will be making more beats like this in various genres.

Send us your tracks! Record them on anything you’d like and send it to us along with a video of your creative process.

We will polish, mix and master and have it released through The Score Magazine on their platform.
